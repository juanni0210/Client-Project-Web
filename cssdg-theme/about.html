About Us

Counselling and Support Services of S.D. & G. is a multi-service, non-profit, accredited organization providing a range of support and services to children, youth, adults and families in the city of Cornwall and the counties of Stormont, Dundas and Glengarry.

We are dedicated to meeting the social and personal needs of the community by providing services that assist people to develop to the full extent of their potential. We strive to achieve this by offering a wide variety of programs and services. It is our goal to promote healthy relationships within the family, in the workplace and the community. Our programs and services include individual, couple and family counselling, an early intervention program, an educational group program, specialized community-based support and an Employee Assistance Program.

Since its inception in 1938, our centre has distinguished itself by providing high-quality support and services under contract with various Ontario Ministries including, the Ministry of Children and Youth Services, Ministry of Community and Social Services and the Ministry of the Attorney General.

Counselling and Support Services of S.D. & G. is a member Agency of Family Service Ontario and the United Way of Stormont, Dundas and Glengarry.
